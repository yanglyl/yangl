About Me
I'm a researcher in biomedical image analysis. In a nutshell, the ultimate goal of my research is to find or help find the answers of biomedical problems from all kinds of biomedical images. My research interests include: multivariate image registration, model-guided image segmentation, neuroimage classification and morphmetric analysis (especially longitudinal), diffusion tensor image (DTI) analysis (DTI image registration and DTI-based brain network analysis) and high-throughput microscope image processing/analysis.
